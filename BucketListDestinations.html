Greece, Europe

There are a lot of cities I would love to visit one day, hopefully soon, but Greece may be at the top of the list. A trip to Greece is a dream for many travelers, with its rich history, stunning landscapes, and vibrant culture. Whether in Santorini, Mykonos, or Crete, the beaches are pristine. The black sand of Santorini and the clear blue waters of Mykonos are truly special. Wandering the streets of Mykonos Town seems like such a dream and just appreciating the scenery. There are plenty of shopping available as well, so you're bound to find something or even take home a souvenior. Island Hopping is also a must. Don’t miss exploring multiple Greek islands—each one has its own unique charm!
    Popular Cities to Visit:
  • Athens
  • Santorini
  • Mykonos
  • Paros


    • NYC, New York

    I have been eager to visit New York City, particularly during the Holidays. Fun fact, I was actually born in New York but my family and I moved when I was only a year old so I never got to really experience it. I would love to see the Christmas lights, decor, the Rockefeller Center Christmas Tree, all of it. Something else to do for fun is taking a trip to Bryant Park. They host one of the most picturesque ice rinks in the city. The rink is free to use (though you’ll need to rent skates), and the surrounding holiday market adds to the festive ambiance.

      Things you can't miss!
    • Grabbing a coffee at Ralph's
    • Times Square
    • Rockefeller Center Christmas Tree
    • Central Park Winter Wonderland



    Bora Bora, French Polynesia

    Bora Bora is a true paradise that feels like something straight out of a dream. Located in the heart of the South Pacific, this stunning island in French Polynesia is known for its crystal-clear turquoise lagoons, overwater bungalows, and breathtaking natural beauty. Whether you're looking for a romantic escape, a luxurious retreat, or simply a place to unwind, Bora Bora offers an idyllic setting with its pristine beaches, vibrant coral reefs, and lush green mountains. Activities such as snorkeling with stingrays, relaxing in a private villa, and enjoying world-class spa treatments make it a perfect destination for relaxation and adventure alike. The island’s peaceful atmosphere, combined with its incredible scenery, makes Bora Bora an unforgettable experience—truly a bucket-list destination for anyone seeking a luxurious tropical getaway.

      Things to do:
    • Stay in an Overwater Bungalow
    • Snorkeling and Diving
    • Sunset Cruise
    • Jet Skiing Around the Island
